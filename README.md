# Shrimp Away! 🦐

> Firefox might not load the game correctly, consider using another browser.

*Note: Instructions may not be entirely truthful.*

An endless runner made for [ComfyJam Summer 2026](https://itch.io/jam/comfy-jam-summer-2026) (theme: **Summer**, limitation: **The Instructions are a lie**). Everything except the CC0 font is handcrafted.

You are a shrimp. You got caught at sea, tossed in a tank, and loaded onto a truck headed far from home. You break free and find yourself rolling down a sunlit beach at full speed. The ocean is in sight, and nothing is going to stop you. **Roll as far as you can and make it back to the sea!**

**Once you get the hang of it, try to secure a spot on the leaderboard!**

## How to Play

Roll down the beach, dodge obstacles, and collect coins. Beat your personal best in meters and compete on the in-game global leaderboard!

| Input | Action |
|-------|--------|
| `A` or `←` | Switch lanes towards the left |
| `D` or `→` | Switch lanes towards the right |
| `Space` | Jump (press again mid-air for a double jump) |

## Power-ups

| Power-up | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| **Magnet** | Attracts coins for a few seconds |
| **Shield** | Restores shrimp carapace armor |
| **Sandwich** | Allows for one double-jump |
